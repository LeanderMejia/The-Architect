FAR SHIPPING OFFICE

Fairshipping Office, situated in Malate, Manila, to revive a heavily criticized yet undoubtedly influential Brutalist style and mold its ideal by modern programmatic needs and appeal. Though Brutalism is opaque in its most common nature, the design feels light and transparent due ti the juxtaposing glass that offers transparency amidst concrete's opacity. This new take on Brutalism makes use of materials in their most honest form embracing the company's commitment to quality service that hopes to stand the test of time.

MANILA WATER

The location of the 1520 sq. m. site for the proposed branch area office is situated at Bonifacio Global City, Taguig. It is a financial district located 11 km (6.8 mi) south-east of the center of Manila in an area disputed between the cities of Makati and Taguig as well as the municipality of Pateros. In recent years, the district has experienced robust commercial growth through the sale of military land by the Bases Conversion and Development Authority (BCDA).

KALIN INC. BUILDING

The idea is to convert the intangibility of the building's linear process through its tangible form. This creates and architecture that creates direct movement and stability of process. The repetition of linear process creates a series of vertical elements that define the volumetric form from exterior to interior. The vertical fins give an interplay between transparency and opacity as well as proper light and ventilation.
