Philippine Architecture and Real Estate Online

▞

July 17, 2016 In News By Ian Fulgar

The Architects embarks on a mission to help entrepreneurs and businesses get into commercial real estate development with charged confidence. We investigate our unique local tropical conditions, arts & culture, growing urban requirements, as well as upcoming parametric design and building technologies to innovate and express the next commercial Filipino architecture.

We offer architectural design, build and real estate Philippines consultancy for commercial building design, rental business, offices, and special buildings like museums, resorts, commercial complex, brand stores, apartment residences, iconic investments, or adaptive reuse requirements.

How We Differ In 5 Ways


1. The Architects offers an impact-oriented approach to our Philippine Architecture projects from concepts to completion. We work closely with clients to not just deliver outputs but to make sure end goals are clear and that we can monitor and evaluate the impact of each project to the lives that matter.

2. We love the local entrepreneurial spirit! Most of our clients are run by family businesses that took generations to evolve. We understand the complexities involved in commercial projects that are sensitive to each member and the next big move they are trying to take collectively.

3. We are strong in technologies with values on building performances. We leverage on various 3D technologies from Parametric Building Information Technologies, Virtual Reality, Simulations Energy Analysis, to Web Programming and Software Development.

4. Our proponent has strong inclination to sustainability practices and have worked with international project standards. We are excited to tender those design experiences, learning, and disciplines to help improve local architecture.

5. We are easy-to-talk to and educated planners, designers, and builders. Our studio fosters academic discussions and encourages potential thought leaders, talents, and scholars from top universities.
