Mission | Vision | Values

The Architects is a pioneering meta-modern architecture and designs firm that explores unique architectural forms influenced by today’s technology, new social circumstances, philosophical juxtapositions, and collaborative spaces. We implement the latest in parametric design technologies, integrated practices, art theories, and business sensibilities in creating fresh approaches and new angles to house designs, commercial property projects, hotels, museums, resorts, condominiums, shopping malls, and future diversified community developments in the Philippines.


OUR MISSION

▞

It’s our mission at Architectural Design Associates to provide client focused service through our responsible practice of Architecture. Our tradition of dedication, professionalism and outstanding customer service is a testament to that mission as we strive each day for excellence in bringing our valued clients’ ideas to life.

OUR VISSION

▞

To be viewed as a respected architectural firm, providing high quality design and services to our clients with honesty and integrity.

OUR VALUES

▞

History Background

The Architects was founded in February of 2018 by Leander M. Mejia, Nichole Pamintuan and Ronna Lopez. Over the past decade The Architectshas completed award winning architectural and planning projects in Philippines. The Architects has a talented staff of designers, administrators and consultants who enjoy working together on projects and creating a spirit of collaboration. In 2013 The Architects renovated, designed and developed their own adaptive mixed-use project and moved to Capas Tarlac.
