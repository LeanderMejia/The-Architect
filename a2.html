MIXED-USE PROJECTS

▞

Mixed-use architecture is the practice of developing buildings and areas that have multiple uses such as residences, hotels, office space, shopping, restaurants, public space, cultural attractions and services such as medical facilities.

BALINTAWAK CLOVERLEAF

The program calls for a mixed-use development with higher concentration of residential blocks and supported by commercial strips in Balintawak, Quezon City. The residential blocks will complement the urban redevelopment of Balintawak Cloverleaf area by addressing a need to absorb and house the existing as well as the projected density of the area.

HIGH GARDENS

As one of the densest cities in the world, Manila is often confronted with the inevitability of building and designing upwards. High Gardens offers a holistic and healthy way of living by creating a self-sustaining ecosystem on its 8100 sqm plot area.

HONGJI GARDENS


Located in the Bay vicinity, the structure aims to mimic the organic character of water, moving away from the cubical shapes of other neighboring developments within the area. The interior design features quality material to maximize the comfort of its users.

EAST TOWN CENTER


The concept aims to reshape the existing shopping center by expanding spaces to create more activation areas that promote entertainment, dining, and leisure which will enrich the development as a whole.
