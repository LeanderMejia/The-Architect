Rosa, Laguna, Eton City makes a strong case for people who want to enjoy the benefits of living in a suburban community, like having access to wide and serene natural spaces, but still want to be close enough to the urban comforts and conveniences that surround the area. GREEN AVENUE


Green Avenue is a 31-hectare master-planned residential development with commercial hub located in General Trias, Cavite, Philippines. It is a community-oriented development with emphasis on green open spaces leading to a lifestyle of wellness through nature. The master plan will be realized in four phases with a total gross floor area of approximately 170,000 square meters - 152,000 of which will be 600 units of residential; 15,000 square meters will be the BPO outlets; 2,000 square meters for retail; and 1,000 square meters are residential amenities.

NEW TAIPEI CITY MUSEUM OF ART


The New Taipei Museum of Art Competition sponsored by the Cultural Affairs Department of the New Taipei City Government called for a design of a multi-arts center adequate for the area and city in an international level. This open ideas competition was launched August 2011 with the goal to come up with a creative and visionary scheme that aids in creating the city’s artistic icon for the new century.
